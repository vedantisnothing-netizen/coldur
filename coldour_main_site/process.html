Making Process

How Coldour ice cream is prepared, step by step.

  1. 1. Select Ingredients

    Fresh milk, cream, sugar and natural flavours are carefully measured.

  2. 2. Pasteurisation

    The mix is heated and quickly cooled to make it safe and smooth.

  3. 3. Churning & Freezing

    The mixture is churned while it is cooled so tiny ice crystals form and air is added.

  4. 4. Adding Flavours & Mix-ins

    Fruit, nuts, sauces, cookies and chocolate are folded into the base.

  5. 5. Packing & Hardening

    The ice cream is packed into cups, cones or tubs and frozen until firm.
