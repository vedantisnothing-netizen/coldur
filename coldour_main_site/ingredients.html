Ingredients Details

Basic base ingredients used in most Coldour ice creams.

Milk & Cream

Fresh dairy milk and cream give ice cream its rich and creamy texture.

Role: Main base for smoothness.

Sugar

Sweetens the mix and helps control the hardness of frozen ice cream.

Role: Sweetness and texture.

Stabilisers

Natural stabilisers like guar gum help keep the texture smooth.

Role: Prevents large ice crystals.

Natural Flavours

Fruit pulps, cocoa, vanilla beans, nuts and extracts add flavour.

Role: Unique taste for each flavour.

Extra Ingredient Notes

• We prefer natural flavours where possible.

• Stabiliser quantity is kept low for a clean taste.

• Nuts are processed in a separate area to reduce cross contact.

• Sugar levels are balanced to keep the ice cream soft but not overly sweet.
