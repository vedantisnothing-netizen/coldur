About Coldour

A small brand with a big love for ice cream.

Coldour started with a simple idea: colourful ice creams that match every mood. We focus on fresh ingredients, balanced sweetness and fun flavour combinations.

Our goal is to make dessert time special for families, friends and anyone who needs a little scoop of happiness.
